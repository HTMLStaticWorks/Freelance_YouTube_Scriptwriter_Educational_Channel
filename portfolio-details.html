THE HIDDEN COST OF SPACE

Solving the Kessler Syndrome through narrative engineering.

THE CHALLENGE

The client wanted to explain the Kessler Syndrome (space junk) without losing the audience in technical jargon. The goal was to maintain a retention rate above 70% for a 20-minute video.

THE STRATEGY

We structured the script as a "ticking clock" narrative. Instead of just stating facts about debris, we framed it as a countdown to the end of modern civilization (GPS, Internet, Communication).

"If we don't fix this by 2035, the sky becomes a wall. And once that wall is up, there's no coming back."

- THE HOOK
